# Dog & Cat Vaccination Cost in Hyderabad: 2026 Pricing Guide

Navigating pet healthcare in Hyderabad can be tricky, so we've broken down exact local costs for dog and cat vaccinations to help you budget.

You’ve brought home a new furry family member, and alongside the immense joy of puppy breath or kitten purrs comes a wave of practical responsibility. One of your first questions is likely about preventative healthcare—specifically, how much you need to budget to keep them safe. 

Understanding the dog vaccination cost in Hyderabad is crucial for planning your pet's first year and beyond. Let's break down the actual costs so you can budget effectively without compromising on the quality of medical care.

## Why Preventative Vaccinations Are Non-Negotiable
Vaccines are not optional add-ons; they actively protect your pets from highly contagious and often fatal diseases. In Hyderabad, where stray populations are high and community dogs frequently interact with pets during neighborhood walks, the risk of environmental exposure to Parvovirus, Distemper, and Rabies is significant. 

Treating a severe case of Parvovirus can cost tens of thousands of rupees, requires days of intensive ICU hospitalization, and carries a high mortality rate. In contrast, the cost of a vaccine is a minor, manageable investment that guarantees your pet's safety.

## Breaking Down Average Dog Vaccination Costs
For a puppy, the first year is the most medically intensive. They require a series of booster shots spaced a few weeks apart to build total immunity. 

*(Disclaimer: Pricing below is for estimation purposes to help you budget. Clinic prices vary based on the pharmaceutical brands used.)*

*   **Anti-Rabies Vaccine (ARV):** ₹300 – ₹500 per dose. Mandatory by law and crucial for human and pet safety.
*   **DHLPPi (7-in-1 or 9-in-1):** ₹800 – ₹1,200 per dose. This is the core combination vaccine that protects against Distemper, Hepatitis, Leptospirosis, Parainfluenza, and Parvovirus. Puppies typically need 3 to 4 doses.
*   **Kennel Cough (Bordetella):** ₹600 – ₹900. Highly recommended if your dog visits grooming salons, dog parks, or boarding facilities.

**Total First-Year Estimate:** You can expect to spend around **₹3,000 to ₹4,500** on your dog's initial vaccination series. After the first year, annual booster appointments drop this cost significantly.

## Breaking Down Average Cat Vaccination Costs
Cats also require strict vaccination schedules, especially if they have access to the outdoors, balconies, or if you plan to foster or adopt more pets in the future.

*   **Anti-Rabies Vaccine (ARV):** ₹300 – ₹500.
*   **Tricat / CRP (3-in-1):** ₹1,000 – ₹1,500 per dose. This core feline vaccine protects against Feline Panleukopenia (feline distemper), Herpesvirus, and Calicivirus.

**Total First-Year Estimate:** Cat owners usually spend between **₹2,500 to ₹3,500** for the initial kitten series. 

## Beware of "Cheap" Vaccines and Cold Chain Failures
If a local dispensary offers prices that seem suspiciously low, you must ask questions. The efficacy of vaccines relies entirely on the "cold chain"—meaning the vials must be kept at a precise, constant temperature from the manufacturer all the way to the syringe. 

Top-tier clinics invest heavily in specialized medical refrigeration and backup generators to survive Hyderabad's power cuts. A compromised, warm vaccine offers zero protection, leaving you with a false sense of security.
