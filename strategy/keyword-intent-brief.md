## Searcher Persona
Pet parents in Hyderabad (primarily dog and cat owners) who view their pets as family members. They worry about the quality of medical care, hidden costs, and whether the veterinarian will be patient and gentle with their anxious pet. To feel confident enough to book an appointment, they need transparent pricing, clear safety protocols, and a compassionate, authoritative tone.
